Bench Press

Category
Strength Training
Muscle Groups
Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
Equipment Needed
Bench, Barbell, Weight Plates
Difficulty
Intermediate

Proper Form

  1. Lie flat on bench, feet flat on ground
  2. Grip barbell slightly wider than shoulder width
  3. Lower bar to chest with controlled movement
  4. Press bar up to starting position

Plank

Category
Core
Muscle Groups
Core, Shoulders, Back
Equipment Needed
Mat (optional)
Difficulty
Beginner

Proper Form

  1. Start in push-up position on forearms
  2. Keep body in straight line from head to heels
  3. Engage core and hold position
  4. Maintain normal breathing

Russian Twist

Category
Core
Muscle Groups
Obliques, Core
Equipment Needed
Medicine Ball (optional)
Difficulty
Intermediate

Proper Form

  1. Sit with knees bent, feet off ground
  2. Lean back slightly, maintaining straight back
  3. Hold weight or clasp hands at chest
  4. Rotate torso side to side
